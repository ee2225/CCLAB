Water

Water's liquid state is essential for life, facilitating various biochemical reactions, transportation of nutrients, and maintenance of environmental conditions conducive to living organisms.

Examples of everyday uses of water:

  • Drinking and hydration
  • Personal hygiene
  • Cleaning
  • Gardening and agriculture
  • Recreation and Leisure

Approximately 71% of the Earth's surface is covered by water. This water exists in various forms, including oceans, seas, lakes, rivers, and glaciers.
